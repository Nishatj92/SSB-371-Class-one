About one-third of her paintings were self-portraits and through her beautiful paintings Frida reaped a sum greater than any of her fellow female artists at that time. Despite the name and fame, Frida deeply struggled in her personal life but attested her strength, perseverance, self-expression and art through perseverance, self-expression and art.
